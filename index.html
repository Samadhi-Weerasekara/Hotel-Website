About Us

Discover Our Underground

Welcome to Ranweli Resort, an enchanting retreat nestled along the picturesque coastline of Sri Lanka. Situated amidst lush tropical gardens and overlooking the azure waters of the Indian Ocean, our resort offers a serene sanctuary where guests can unwind and reconnect with nature. Discover a harmonious blend of modern comforts and traditional charm in our thoughtfully designed accommodations. From cozy rooms to spacious villas, each offering breathtaking views and contemporary amenities to ensure a relaxing stay. Indulge your senses with delectable cuisine inspired by local flavors and international influences at our on-site restaurants. Savor freshly caught seafood, authentic Sri Lankan dishes, and tantalizing global favorites crafted by our skilled chefs.
